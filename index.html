Research Focus

Practical environmental science for cleaner water systems.

Dr. Zainabi's research focuses on waste management, wastewater treatment, and plant-based remediation systems, including SHEFROL bioreactor applications for greywater and sewage treatment.

His work includes household, village, and pilot-plant systems, with emphasis on low-cost treatment methods suited to real-world environmental and public health needs.
